Weather app

Using this app, you can search the name of any city and receive the current temperature in celcius and fahrenheit. It will also show you the temperature of the next 5 days, at the exact same time of the day. This is not ideal! The app will also show the country in which that city is located. You can change the country by typing the two-letter code of that country following a comma and a space after the city name.

Parent Support Group

As a parent of deaf children, you can connect with other parents and Deaf nannies. We believe that connecting with others who share your social identity helps you feel belonged, less alone and more confident. You can also ask your questions and get practical help easier. Most importantly, however, your child will be in a rich language environment which is essential to their healthy development
